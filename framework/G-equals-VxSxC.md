# G = V × S × C — The ULG Governance Formula

## Ultra-Light Governance Core Framework

**G = V × S × C**

| Symbol | Term | Definition |
|--------|------|------------|
| **G** | Governance (治理) | The system's overall governance capacity — its ability to coordinate, sustain, and deliver |
| **V** | Velocity / Behavioral Density (行為密度) | The rate at which effective actions are completed, progressed, or iterated per unit of time |
| **S** | Space (空間因子) | The degree to which participants share understanding, decision-making, and goal spaces |
| **C** | Connection / Nodes (節點因子) | The design quality of relay points where tasks, decisions, and accountability transfer between participants |

---

## Traditional Interpretation (Pre-AI)

In traditional governance:
- **V** = Human behavioral density only
- **S** = Physical and organizational space shared by humans
- **C** = Meeting points, handoffs, approvals — all human-to-human

## AI-Era Redefinition

In the age of AI teams, the formula evolves:

> **(Human × AI) Hybrid Velocity × (Shared Decision Space) × (Designed Collaboration Nodes) = System Flow Rate**

Simplified:

> **Hybrid-V × Shared-S × Designed-C = Flow**

Flow is not speed. Flow is:

> The ability of humans and AI to naturally progress tasks with minimal friction.

---

## The Three Critical Nodes

In any Human-AI team, the most important nodes are:

1. **Problem Definition Node** — Human-led
   - Humans define what the problem actually is
   
2. **AI Production Node** — AI-led
   - AI generates, analyzes, simulates, and iterates at high speed
   
3. **Re-structuring Node** — Human-led
   - Humans reorganize, calibrate, and make final judgment calls

These three nodes form the fundamental operating cycle:

> **Human defines → AI advances → Human restructures → AI extends → Human judges (completion)**

This is the core operating map for all hybrid teams.

---

## Key Principles

1. **Structure beats effort** (結構大於努力)
2. **Position beats direction** (位置大於方向)
3. **Fewer nodes → faster system** (節點少 → 系統快)
4. **Clear nodes → stable system** (節點清晰 → 系統穩)
5. **Correct nodes → system completes** (節點正確 → 系統能完成)
6. **AI completes work, but never completes responsibility** (AI 可以完成工作，但不能完成責任)
